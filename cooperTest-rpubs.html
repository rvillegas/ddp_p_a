Definitions

VO2 Max VO2 max is the maximal oxygen uptake or the maximum volume of oxygen that can be utilized in one minute during maximal or exhaustive exercise. It is measured as milliliters of oxygen used in one minute per kilogram of body weight.

Cooper Test

Cooper test is an easy way to calculate the VO2 Max, and know the aerobic condition of the runner

Cooper Test

The test consist to measure the distance in meters in 12 minutes. With this information, the VO2Max can be calculated with the following formula:

VO2 max = 22,351 * distance(m)/1000 - 11,288

With the VO2 max, the age and sex the aerobic condition can be determined.

Example

An example using this test, my data is:

Age:53 Sex: Male Distance:2400

distance=2400
VO2Max<-22.351 * distance/1000 - 11.288

The VO2 Max is 42.354
